About Somnath Temple

Somnath Temple, located in Prabhas Patan near Veraval in Gujarat, is one of the most sacred Jyotirlinga shrines of Lord Shiva. It has a rich history, having been destroyed and rebuilt several times over centuries. The present temple, reconstructed in Chalukya style of architecture, stands as a symbol of resilience and faith.
