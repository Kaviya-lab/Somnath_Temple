DivAura is a multilingual, AI- and IoT-powered web application designed to improve crowd management and devotee experience at major pilgrimage sites such as Somnath, Dwarka, Ambaji, and 
Pavagadh. 
The system integrates virtual queuing, smart ticketing, real-time crowd monitoring, and AI-based prediction models to ensure safer, smoother, and more organized movement of devotees.

By combining machine learning, sensor-based monitoring, and web technologies, DivAura forecasts crowd size and waiting times, detects potential risks such as falls or overcrowding, and

The platform offers dedicated support for elderly and differently-abled devotees while helping authorities optimize parking, staffing, and traffic flow.
